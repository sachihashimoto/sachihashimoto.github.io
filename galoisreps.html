Galois representations learning seminar:

Spring 2024

This is a learning seminar at Brown University to study galois representations attached to elliptic curves. We meet 3-3:50pm in Kassar 205. We will read the two papers:
Serre - Propriétés galoisiennes des points d'ordre fini des courbes elliptiques
Mazur - Rational Isogenies of Prime Degree
